# STV RADIOMELTDOWN
**Lithonia, GA · Culture. Street. Sound.**

A mobile-first, web-based radio network with four distinct stations. No app install required — scan a QR, open a browser, and you're in.

## Who This Is For

**Primary listener:** Urban music fans 18–40 who want curated, mood-based playlists with real cultural identity — not just an algorithm. Rooted in the Atlanta/Lithonia community.

**By station:**
- **STV Red** — Hip-hop and street culture fans, everyday listeners
- **STV Gold** — Polished R&B and soul, slightly older or more premium taste
- **STV Fit** — Active listeners who need high-energy sets for workouts and runs
- **Love & Co.** — Late night, couples, slow jam and neo-soul fans

---

## The Customer

The listener is the audience. The customer — the one who pays — is:

- Local and regional businesses reaching Atlanta's urban demographic via audio ads or sponsored drops
- Music artists and independent labels seeking placement and promotion
- Event promoters in Atlanta and the Southeast
- Travel brands via the Faire Fare affiliate funnel (Expedia, Hotels.com, Vrbo)
- Fitness brands and gyms aligned with STV Fit's listener base

---

## 12 Ways to Monetize

### Advertising
1. **Spot Ads / Audio Drops** — 15–30 sec paid placements between tracks, sold per rotation or monthly
2. **Station Sponsorships** — "STV Fit brought to you by [brand]" — flat monthly fee for station ownership
3. **Player Page Banner Ads** — Visual placements on the web player with high mobile visibility

### Artist & Label Services
4. **Paid Playlist Placement** — Artists pay to be added to rotation, tiered by station and frequency
5. **Premiere Slots** — Featured "Now Playing" hero placement when a station loads
6. **Station IDs / Radio Edits** — Custom recorded drops or edits produced for artists

### Creator & Campaign Economy
7. **QR Code Campaigns** — Brands pay for a custom QR linking to a sponsored station or campaign page
8. **White-Label Custom Stations** — Branded player builds for events, product launches, or campaigns

### Travel & Affiliate
9. **Affiliate Commissions** — Expedia, Hotels.com, and Vrbo links convert engaged listeners into travel bookings
10. **Travel Package Promotions** — Curated packages promoted during matching programming (e.g., Love & Co. → couples getaways)

### Subscription & Community
11. **Listener Membership (Gold Tier)** — Ad-free listening, exclusive sets, early drops for paying subscribers
12. **Live Events & Virtual Parties** — Ticketed listening parties and live DJ streams on Kick and YouTube

## The Big Picture

The distribution layer is already built — four stations, QR codes, and cross-platform presence on Kick, YouTube, and TikTok. The product is the audience. The travel affiliate integration proves the model works. Next move: direct advertisers and a listener membership tier.
