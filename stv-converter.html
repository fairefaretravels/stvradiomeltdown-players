Not sure which format to pick?
MP3
Best for music and podcasts. Plays everywhere. Smaller file size.
WAV
Uncompressed audio. Best for recording studios and editing.
AAC / M4A
Apple's format. Great quality at smaller sizes. Good for phones.
FLAC
Lossless — full quality, no compression. Bigger file, perfect sound.
MP4
Standard video. Works on phones, web, social media, everywhere.
MOV
Apple's video format. Best for editing in iMovie or Final Cut.
WEBM
Web-optimized video. Smaller file, great for websites and streaming.
GIF
Short animated clip, no sound. Good for social media loops.
